# **Mexico**

## **Quesadilla**

![Quesadilla](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/53f03dd1e4b027cf34f2fc4b/791d5445-ccdf-4845-be95-3e8212943f8a/chicken+quesadillas.jpg?format=2500w)

### Ingredients

- 8 small flour or corn tortillas

- 1 cup (150 grams) warm shredded chicken (approx. 1-2 Chicken Breasts)

- 2 cups (225 to 250 grams) Mexican Blend shredded cheese

- 1 Medium tomato (diced)

- 1 Onion (diced)

- 1-2 peppers (diced)

- Cilantro *(Coriander)* to taste

### Steps

1. Heat a pan (medium)

2. Place one tortilla on the pan.

3. Layer all the ingredients on the tortilla, beginning with the cheese, shredded chicken, and other toppings, and cover it with another tortilla

4. Let cook and flip over using the pancake turner. Cook long enough to make both tortillas crunchy without burning and melting the cheese.

5. Remove from the pan, place the quesadilla on a cutting board, and cut it into 4 pieces.

6. Place on a plate with Salsa Fresca, Guacamole, and sour cream, and ENJOY!
