# Turkey

## Mücver

### Ingredients

 - 2–2.5 kg zucchini
- 5 eggs
- 2–2.5 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 large bunch fresh dill (finely chopped)
- 5–6 green onions (finely chopped)
- 3 cloves garlic (crushed)
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
- Vegetable oil (for frying)

### Steps

1. Grate the zucchini and squeeze out as much liquid as possible using your hands or a clean cloth.
2. In a large bowl, combine the grated zucchini and eggs. Mix well.
3. Add chopped dill, green onions, and garlic.
4. Season with salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes.
5. Gradually add the flour and mix until you get a batter that is thick enough to hold together but still soft enough to scoop with a spoon
6. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
7. Drop spoonfuls of the mixture into the pan and gently flatten them.
8. Fry both sides until golden brown.
10. Transfer to paper towels to remove excess oil
