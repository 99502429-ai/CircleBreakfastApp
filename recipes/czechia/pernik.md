# Czech Republic

## Perník

![Perník](https://www.cooklikeczechs.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/gingerbread-slice.jpg)

### Ingredients

- ¼ tsp ground cloves or 10 whole
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon or 1 cinnamon stick
- ½ tsp ground allspice or 10 whole
- 2 anise stars
- ½ tsp whole fennel seed
- ½ tsp whole anise seeds
- 1½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 3 tbl unsweetened cocoa powder (sifted to remove clumps)
- 2 large eggs
- ⅓ cup oil
- 1 cup milk
- ¼ cup walnuts


### Steps

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Place all the spices in coffee or hand grinder. Grind into a powder.
3. Mix spices with remaining ingredients (except the walnuts) until batter is smooth. Stir in walnuts.
4. Bake for 30 minutes.
5. Let cool and top with sifted powdered sugar or chocolate frosting.
